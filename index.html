About my studio

Jia Huang is an independent musician who learnt classical piano from age seven. She has since then explored many of her music interests, including A cappella, played as keyboard in local band (Rock band "Startup", Funk bank "Yolk"). Most recently, in Spring 2022 she completed the Film scoring class from Berklee College of Music.



"To share my observations and feelings of the world to you through music."
